Not sure how to find your profile url?

On the steam client

  1. Log into the steam client
  2. Hover over your username in the menu
  3. In the dropdown that appears, select profile
  4. Copy the link that appears under the menu, starting with "https://steamcommunity.com/profiles"

On the steam website

  1. Log into your steam account
  2. Hover over your username in the menu
  3. In the dropdown that appears, select profile
  4. Copy the link in the url bar in your browser

Paste that link in the box on the home page
